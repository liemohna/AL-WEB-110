Projects

Blaine & Associates, Inc. is a mid-sized, full-service architectural firm located in Ann Arbor, Michigan. We provide services to a diverse group of clients in southeast Michigan and throughout the country.

We employ 20 architects, interior designers and support staff who provide architectural services, pre-design consultation, development services, interior design, commercial office space planning, renovations, and institutional work.

Founded in 1987 by Kelly Blaine, the firm was established around four basic principles:

  • Dedication to client service
  • Commitment to system engineering concepts
  • Development of sophisticated design
  • Awareness of user needs
